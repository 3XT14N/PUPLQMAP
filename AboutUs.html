About Us

Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) is a premier state university in the Philippines, renowned for its commitment to providing accessible and quality education to a diverse student population. Established in 1904, PUP has a long and storied history, making it one of the country's oldest and most respected educational institutions.

"MISSION"

PUP's mission is to nurture the intellectual, social, ethical, and spiritual dimensions of individuals by providing them with a well-rounded education. The university aims to produce competent and socially responsible graduates who can contribute significantly to the nation's progress and development.

"VISSION"

PUP envisions becoming a globally recognized center of excellence in higher education, distinguished for its innovative programs, cutting-edge research, and transformative impact on society. It aspires to be a leading force in shaping the future of education in the Philippines
